About the Authors

By Daniela Franceschetti, Joanne Guerra, Katie Iwagami, and Sam Schlinkert

This website was created by Daniela Franceschetti, Joanne Guerra, Katie Iwagami, and Sam Schlinkert for our final group website project in Pratt Institute's School of Information.

Daniela Franceschetti

Daniela Franceschetti is a student in Pratt Institute's School of Information. Her interests include storytelling, creativity, and collaborative world-building through live action role-playing. For this project, she contributed to the development of the website's content and overall educational focus.

Joanne Guerra

Joanne Guerra is a student in Pratt Institute's School of Information. In addition to her graduate studies, she works in real estate and is interested in land use, property ownership, and how people build long-term stability through housing and community development. For this project, she contributed to the website's research, writing, and organization.

Katie Iwagami

Katie Iwagami is a first-year student in the MSLIS program at Pratt Institute's School of Information. This is her first group website project and her second assignment using GitHub. For this project, she contributed to the website's content development and collaborative design process.

Sam Schlinkert

Sam Schlinkert is a student in Pratt Institute's School of Information and an experienced coder. For this project, Sam contributed technical knowledge, coding support, and helped strengthen the functionality and structure of the website.
